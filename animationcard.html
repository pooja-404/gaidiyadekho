Factors on which premium depend

Insured Declared Value (IDV)

The IDV represents the maximum claim amount that the insurance company will pay if the vehicle is stolen or damaged beyond repair.

Cubic Capacity (CC of Engine)

The engine's cubic capacity influences the premium, with larger engines generally leading to higher premiums.

Geographical Zones

Different zones may have different risk levels, which can impact the premium. Urban areas usually have higher premiums due to increased risk.

Vehicle Manufacturing Year

Older vehicles generally attract lower premiums due to their lower IDV, but they might have higher maintenance costs.
