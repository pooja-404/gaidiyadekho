Insurance Claim Procedure

Own Damage Claim

Inform the insurance company and police as soon as possible. Do not remove the vehicle from the accident site without permission. If you have a cashless policy, park the vehicle at an authorized workshop for repairs.

Theft Claim

File an FIR for the theft and inform the insurance company immediately. Submit all required documents, including the claim form and vehicle details. If the vehicle is not recovered, the amount will be paid within 7 days after approval.

General Claim Procedure

If you are a victim in an accident, gather all information about the insurance policy and notify the insurance holder. Ensure that all relevant details are shared with the insurance company.
