• Cancellation of insurance

    The policy can be cancelled by insurance company. The company sends the seven days notice to the insurance holder. The insurance company will refund the premium for remaining period if there is no claim under the policy.

    A policy can be cancelled only after ensuring that the vehicle is insured with any other company. The original certificate of insurance must be submitted to the company.

    The insurance company should inform RTA (regional transport authority) for such cancellation.
